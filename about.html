Who We Are

More Than a Factory —
We're Your Production Partner

Xelnt Traders was founded on a simple belief: independent streetwear brands deserve the same quality manufacturing that the big players get — without the gatekeeping.

Based in Sialkot, Pakistan's manufacturing capital, we've spent 15+ years perfecting the art of custom streetwear production. We don't just make clothes — we help brand builders bring their creative vision to life with precision, quality, and respect.

Today, we serve brands across the USA, UK, Europe, and Africa — with a special focus on supporting Black-owned streetwear labels. Because representation in the supply chain matters just as much as representation on the rack.

Our Journey

Milestones

2009
Founded in Sialkot

Started as a small cut-and-sew workshop with 10 machines and a dream to make quality accessible.

2013
First International Client

Landed our first US-based streetwear brand. Manufactured their debut hoodie collection of 200 pieces.

2016
Expanded to 50+ Machines

Moved to a larger facility in the Sialkot Industrial Estate. Added embroidery and screen printing in-house.

2019
200+ Brand Partners

Reached 200 brand clients worldwide. Introduced DTG printing and sublimation capabilities.

2022
Solar Installation

Installed solar panels powering 60% of our facility. Committed to sustainable manufacturing.

2024
500+ Brands Served

Surpassed 500 brand partnerships across 40+ countries with 50K+ monthly production capacity.
